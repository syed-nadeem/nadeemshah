Zain eSIM

In my role, I spearheaded the development of a transformative eSIM purchase and activation system for our company's customers, seamlessly integrating with both our core systems and key third-party platforms, including the National login system (Nafath). This project was architected using a sophisticated stack: Laravel for a robust backend, Livewire for dynamic front-end interactions, FilamentPHP for an intuitive admin panel, Alpine.js for reactive components, and TailwindCSS for a modern, responsive design. This initiative not only enhanced customer experience by offering a streamlined digital solution for eSIM management but also showcased my ability to navigate complex integrations and deliver a high-quality, scalable solution.

Personal Portfolio

For my personal portfolio site, I embraced a modern tech stack to ensure a seamless user experience and dynamic functionality. Utilizing Laravel, I laid down a robust backend framework, while Livewire facilitated an interactive front-end without compromising on speed. FilamentPHP was chosen for its elegant administration panel capabilities, enhancing the site's management efficiency. Alpine.js brought in the reactivity and minimalism needed for smooth interactions, and TailwindCSS provided the stylistic flexibility to craft a unique and responsive design. This project not only showcases my technical expertise but also my ability to integrate various technologies for a cohesive and efficient digital solution.
